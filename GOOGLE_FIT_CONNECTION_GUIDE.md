## How It Works

The app uses **Health Connect** (Android's unified health platform) which syncs with Google Fit. When you connect Google Fit to Health Connect on your device, the app can access that data.

---

## Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Install Health Connect (Android 14+)
1. Open Google Play Store
2. Search for "Health Connect"
3. Install the Health Connect app by Google
4. Open Health Connect and complete initial setup

### Step 2: Connect Google Fit to Health Connect
1. Open the **Google Fit** app on your device
2. Make sure you're signed in with **eng.yousif1357@gmail.com**
3. Go to Google Fit Settings
4. Find "Connected apps" or "Health Connect"
5. Enable connection to Health Connect
6. Grant permissions for:
   - Steps
   - Heart Rate
   - Calories
   - Sleep
   - Workouts

### Step 3: Connect in Saheeh App
1. Open the Saheeh app
2. Go to **Settings** → **Health & Fitness** → **Wearable & Google Fit**
3. Tap **"Connect"** under Google Fit section
4. Grant permissions when prompted:
   - Steps
   - Heart Rate
   - Active Energy Burned
   - Sleep in Bed
   - Workout
   - Distance
5. Tap **"Sync Now"** to sync today's data

## Automatic XP Awards

When data is synced, the app automatically awards XP:
